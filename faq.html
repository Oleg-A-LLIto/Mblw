Once an order is placed, you have a small window of 30 minutes to cancel it before it processes to the warehouse. Once the order processes and is accepted by the warehouse, you may still request cancellation, however please know that cancellation at that stage is not guaranteed.
The best way to tell if your order can be cancelled is the availability of the cancellation button. If your order is able to be cancelled, there will be a cancellation button available in the order on your My Account Page. You have the option to cancel the entire invoice, or you can cancel select items.
